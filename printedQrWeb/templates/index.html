printedQr

Carry your QR Code with you

About

printedQr is about 3D printing data, not only stuff
You can carry your data, fisically, in a 3D printed Qr code, for example, an incredibly cool necklace containing your twitter url.

Try it online!
This will only download the SCAD file, using the not-online version would give you the STL files, ready to slice
